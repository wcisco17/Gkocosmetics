What is Cutaneous aging?

Gkocosmetics & Medspa proposes its expertise for a personalized treatment of cutaneous aging. Cutaneous aging is just part of it, it depends both on intrinsic factors, factors that are genetically programmed, and extrinsic factors, such as sun exposure, pollution, smoking, stress, nutrition etc..

Hyperpigmentation

Solar Lentigenes - widely known as ‘sun spots,’ ‘age spots’ and ‘liver spots,’ solar lentigenes are freckles caused by sun exposure. Melasma - Often known as the ‘pregnancy mask,’ melasma is caused by hormonal changes due to pregnancy and is characterized by splotchy, pigmented areas usually on the face (except the eye area). There are various causes of hyperpigmentation, including overexposure to the sun, sun damage, heredity, picking at the skin, hormonal and changes certain medications.

Acne

Nobody is completely sure what causes acne. Experts believe the primary cause is a rise in androgen levels - androgen is a type of hormone. Androgen levels rise when a human becomes an adolescent. Rising androgen levels make the oil glands under your skin grow; the enlarged gland produces more oil. Excessive sebum can break down cellular walls in your pores, causing bacteria to grow.
